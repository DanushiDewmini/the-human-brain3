Introduction of Neuroscience and Brain Function

Neuroscience is the scientific study of the nervous system (the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nervous system), its functions, and its disorders.

How the Brain Adapts and Changes

Throughout life, we encounter changing environments that require us to learn and adapt. Human brain plasticity describes the capacity of our brain to change in response to these experiences. The brain is most sensitive to experience during childhood, when it is changing most dramatically.

Illustration of neuroplasticity in the brain.

  • Types of Neuroplaaticity :
    • Function Plasticity : The brain's ability to move function from damaged areas to undamaged areas.
    • Structural Plasticity : The brain's ability to physically change its structure in response to learning and expeiroence.
  • Factors Influencing Neurosplasticity :
    • Age : Young brains are generally more plastic than older ones.
    • Experience : Learning new skills can enhance brain plasticity.
